Before beginning please run the "make"(without quotation marks)
command in the terminal in the same directory.

Q1. Simply run-> ./req mm/dd/yyyy
where mm/dd/yyyy is command line argument to display the calendar.

Q2. Simply run-> ./new2 command1 argument1 argument2 ..argumentn
where command1 -> add, sub, mul, div, exp
argument[i] is the number.

Q3.a) Simply run-> ./bst and follow the commands in the console

Q3.b) Simply run-> ./avl and follow the commands in the console

Q4. Simply run-> ./sort and follow the commands in the console
